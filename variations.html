Variations

Random Article to Jesus

In random article to Jesus, you need to start from a Random Article and make your way to the
Jesus article in five clicks.

Random Article to Random Article

In Random article to random article, you start from a Random Article and navigate your way to
another Random Article

Wikipedia Maze

In the wikipedia maze, you need to go from one article to another, but during your trip,
you need to go via other different Random Articles, you can go through as many different other sites as you'd like . In this variation, I would recommend using CTRL + F to search for things across websites to prevent the game lasting hours.
You also may want to only use articles to do with things that you are familiar with to decrease the difficulty.
